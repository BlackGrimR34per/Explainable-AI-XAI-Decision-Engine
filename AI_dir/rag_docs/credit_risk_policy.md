Applicants with CTOS scores above 750 are classified as low credit risk.
CTOS scores between 650-750 indicate moderate risk and require additional review.
CTOS scores below 650 are considered high risk.

Applicants with more than 3 late payments in the past 12 months face increased scrutiny.
Zero late payments with good repayment history strengthens approval likelihood.

Total credit utilization above 70% indicates potential financial stress.
Credit utilization below 30% demonstrates responsible credit management.
